Odezenne

Odezenne is a French alternative band that I’ve been listening to for a very long time. For this project, I created a box based on the band’s musical and textual universe. So I came up with a fictiv merch including several editions, stickers, posters and three vinyl records with the thirty-two tracks from their Live album. I really enjoyed working around something so abstract as musical identity. The boxed set contains: Three editions; Odezenne le rap ovniesque, Merci Odezene, Live. A thirty-two photo serie associated with each track, three vinyl records, a flipbook, a bottle of whisky, a concert ticket, four posters and a silkscreen print.
